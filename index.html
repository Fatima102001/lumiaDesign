About Me

I’m the founder of Lumia Design, a creative space built around my passion for branding and eye-catching visuals.
I specialize in visual identity, social media content, and creative projects that help brands stand out. My goal is to turn ideas into unique, authentic designs that tell stories and capture the essence of each brand’s vision.
Since childhood, I dreamed of creating something that truly belonged to me—something people would recognize and associate with my unique style. As I grew, I discovered my passion for design and decided to build my brand: Lumia Design. The name reflects light, creativity, and clarity—values that guide every project I work on.
Lumia Design embodies both my creative vision and my roots—merging passion, design, and identity into a name that lasts.
If you’re looking for a designer to bring your brand to life, let’s collaborate!
